The following commands are available. All take <victim> as an arguement:

shoot:  Shoots the <victim>. 

guillotine: Uses the guillotine on the <victim>!

stab: Runs <victim> through with a saber!

hug: Hugs the <victim>. Wait, why are they a victim again?

slap: Slaps the <victim>.

trout: Slaps the <victim> around a bit with a large trout.

crossbow: Shoots the <victim> with a crossbow!
